The Verma Family

Abhishek Verma

Abhishek Verma was born on 23rd of February 1968 in New Delhi. He is the son of Shrikant Verma and Veena Verma.

Verma graduated from Hindu College, Delhi University, with Masters Diploma from Georgetown University, Washington DC.

Verma is a successful global industrialist and philanthropist. He is the chairman of the Verma Family Office, a family office that manages the family's investments and philanthropic activities.

Abhishek Verma, is a well known global industrialist and philanthropist. He is the Chairman of the Board of several companies that are aligned with Government of India’s ‘Make In India’ policy and these companies are engaged in businesses across 4 continents.

Abhishek Verma heads the Private Office of Verma Family, a family office that manages the family's investments and philanthropic activities globally.

Abhishek Verma is married to Anca Verma, a Romanian entrepreneur and a well known TV celebrity from România and they have a daughter Nicolle Verma.
