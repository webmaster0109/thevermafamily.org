The Verma Family

Veena Verma

Veena Verma was born in 1941 in Alwar Rajasthan. Her grandfather was the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Alwar.

Mrs Veena Verma married Shrikant Verma in January 1967. Veena Verma entered politics after the death of her husband in 1986.

She was elected to the Indian Parliament (Rajya Sabha) in 1986 as a member of the Congress party. Verma served as a Member of Parliament for three terms. Verma is a strong advocate for women's rights and empowerment.

She also worked on issues such as education, healthcare, and poverty alleviation. Veena Verma lives with her son in Delhi.
